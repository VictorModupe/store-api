Our Story

Founded by experienced traders who understood the challenges of accessing capital, MBTC was created to bridge the gap between talented traders and trading opportunities.

We believe that trading skill shouldn't be limited by capital constraints. Our mission is to provide a fair, transparent, and trader-friendly platform where talent meets opportunity.
