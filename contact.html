Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get funded?

+

Once you pass your challenge, funding typically takes 1-2 business days. We process all successful evaluations quickly to get you trading as soon as possible.

What happens if I fail the challenge?

+

If you don't meet the challenge requirements, you can retake it at a discounted rate. We also provide detailed feedback to help you improve your trading strategy.

Are there any time limits on the challenge?

+

No, we don't impose artificial time limits. Take the time you need to demonstrate your trading skills properly. Quality over speed is our philosophy.

How often can I withdraw profits?

+

Profit withdrawals are processed bi-weekly. You can request a withdrawal every two weeks, and we'll process it within 1-3 business days.
